Lesson 11

Grammar - Když je budoucnost jistá

Když se studentů zeptám, jak vyjádřit v angličtině budoucnost, většinou mi odpoví, že přece pomocí will. Není to sice špatná odpověď, ale zachycuje to jen jednu z možností, jak se anglicky dá mluvit o minulosti a dokonce ani ne tu nejčastěji používanou. 😅 Jak je to tedy?

Dobrou zprávou je, že se pro vyjádření budoucnosti nemusíte učit žádné nové složité časy ani další tvary sloves. Dokonce to jde až tak daleko, že si vystačíte s časy, které již umíte. To, co je pro správné vyjádření budoucnosti nejdůležitější, je míra jistoty či nejistoty mluvčího.

🔥 Přítomný průběhový, když jsme si stoprocentně jistí 🔥

Pokud jsme již napevno rozhodnutí, co budeme v budoucnosti dělat, použijeme přítomný průběhový. Může to sice znít zvláštně, ale pokud uvedeme správný časový kontext, dodá to našemu proslovu na rozhodnoti. O víkendu prostě dělám tohle a přes to nejede vlak, takže se mi nemotej do plánů.

🧩 I am attending a workshop on advanced firewall protection strategies next weekend. = Jsme pevně rozhodnutí, že tam jdeme.

🧩 In October, our company is launching a new AI-powered product that aims to revolutionize the industry. = Už je to na spadnutí a určitě se stane.

🧩 To resolve the ongoing system glitches, I am implementing new error-handling protocols in the software tomorrow. = zítra to mám jako prioritu na programu dne

🧩 I am updating our website to make it more user-friendly next week. = mám to napevno v plánu

Přítomný průběhový můžeme také s úspěchem aplikovat na situace, kde je jistota budoucnosti dána tím, že jsme si udělali nějakou rezervaci, třeba se objednali k lékaři, nebo okolí nás bezpodmínečně do něčeho tlačí, například když máme povinný meeting se šéfem a nedá se z toho vykroutit. Těmto situacím se anglicky říká arrangement a můžete si to představit jako nalinkovanou (naaranžovanou) budoucnost, která se určitě stane. Někdy je rozdíl mezi naším pevným rozhodnutím a takovýmto arrangement je velmi malý nebo žádný.

🧩 I'm meeting my boss in the afternoon. He said that he wants to discuss something important with me. = Brr, to zní strašně a nemůžu se z toho vykroutit! 😅

🧩 He's leaving tomorrow in the morning to attend PyCon. = konference má pevně daný termín, a tak musíme ve tři vyrazit, abychom to stihli

🧩 Microsoft is releasing a new update next week. = pevný arrangement

🧩 I'm seeing my dentist this Wednesday. = mám rezervovaný termín

🕔 Přítomný prostý, když se něco v budoucnosti opakuje 🕔

Dalším velmi častým typem je vyjádření budoucnosti pomocí přítomného času prostého, což asi zní ještě zvláštěji než průběh, ale ve skutečnosti jde o zcela logickou záležitost. Představte si kupříkladu kino, které má naplánováno, které filmy bude hrát, a to opakuje třeba dva týdny v kuse. Nebo vlak, který jezdí pořád stejně, protože se řídí jízdním řádem.

🧩 The system automatically backs up data every Sunday night. = Je teprve úterý a já čekám na backup, který z mého pohledu sice je v budoucnosti, ale děje se pravidelně.

🧩 The maintenance window for the servers is every third Saturday of the month. = Údržba serveru proběhne v budoucnosti, ale probíhá pravidelně ve stejnou dobu.

🧩 The password reset link expires in 24 hours. = I když nám heslo teprve vyprší, jde o nastavené pravidlo, které se chová pořád stejně.

🧩 My train leaves at 10:30. = Pro mě je to sice budoucnost, ale vlak jezdí v tyto časy opakovaně a následující měsíce bude.

🧩 What time do you finish your work tomorrow? - I finish at five. = Sice se ptám na zítřek, ale kolega má pevnou pracovní dobu.

Vocabulary - plánovací slovíčka

O slovíčko schedule už jsme zavadili, ale jen v kontextu jeho správné výslovnosti. Protože Britové velmi rádi plánují, mají pro vyjádření plánů opravdu bohatou slovní zásobu. Protože se pohybujete ve světě IT a businessu, nejspíš mnoho z nich slýcháte na denní bázi a máte v nich tak trochu guláš. Pojďme si je tedy projít. 😁

⏰ Calendar ⏰

A system of organizing and measuring time, often in the form of a chart or table, that outlines days, weeks, and months. It refers to the representation of time, where events, tasks, or appointments can be marked.

🧩 Mark your availability on the team calendar, so we can schedule the next project meeting.

🧩 I marked the holiday dates on my calendar.

🧩 My calendar reminds me of upcoming appointments.

⏰ Schedule ⏰

A plan of procedures or events and the time at which they are intended to occur. It is a broader term that can refer to any planned series of events or tasks along with their intended times.

🧩 The construction project is on schedule and expected to be completed by the end of the month.

🧩 I have a busy schedule today with a lot of meetings.

🧩 Can you check the schedule to see when our break is?

⏰ Agenda ⏰

A list of items to be discussed or accomplished at a meeting or event. It is specific to meetings or events and outlines discussion points or tasks.

🧩 Before the meeting began, the manager distributed the agenda so everyone could prepare for the discussion topics.

🧩 The meeting agenda lists three main topics for discussion.

🧩 Before we start, does everyone have a copy of the agenda?

⏰ Itinerary ⏰

A detailed plan or route of a journey. It is specific to journeys and includes locations, dates, and times for travel-related activities.

🧩 The travel agent sent over our itinerary, detailing the flights, accommodations, and tours planned for our vacation.

🧩 Our holiday itinerary includes a visit to the museum and a boat tour.

🧩 I checked the itinerary to see which city we are visiting next.

⏰ Timetable ⏰

A schedule of the times of arrivals and departures or occurrences of events. It is often used for fixed times for public transportation, classes, or recurring events.

🧩 The train timetable is available on the Internet.

🧩 The students checked the exam timetable to see when their tests were scheduled.

🧩 I need to check the bus timetable to catch the earliest one.

Listening - How ChatGPT works?

Meet John, a software developer. John develops a program and now he realizes the program is surrounded by a lot of bugs. John starts exploring a solution; he surfs through the internet, checks programmer communities, doubting every step of the way. John feels his problem is not solved. Desperate to find the solution, John meets his friend Adam, who comes up with an idea of an artificially intelligent and practical solution called ChatGPT.

Adam says ChatGPT has the caliber to systematically resolve all the bugs with an elaborate explanation for every step it makes. ChatGPT is an AI trained model that works in a conversational way, developed by OpenAI. Fascinated by hearing this, John asks Adam to explain it to him in detail. After Adam explains, John learns that GPT, AKA Generative Pretrained Transformer, has come a long way before the introduction of ChatGPT. In the past, natural language processing used to deal with a specific task with large amounts of data. GPT was first released in 2018, which contained 117 millions parameters. GPT-2 and GPT-3 each one is stronger than the one before.

The main reason why GPT received little attention was that it was more of an idea or task than a finished product. But after the introduction of GPT-2, it gained a lot of attention as it could accurately predict the word that would begin a text. Then they introduced GPT-3, which is a strong language model achieving translation, question-answering, and performing three-digit arithmetic, all compared to all other achievements of OpenAI.

So how does ChatGPT work? ChatGPT uses deep learning techniques to generate human-like text. It is based on the machine learning model derived from the class called the large language model. ChatGPT is a byproduct of InstructGPT. InstructGPT introduced a strategy for integrating human feedback into the training process to match model outputs. This innovative technology made ChatGPT exceptional. It is trained on massive datasets of text from the internet and learns from the patterns and relationships between words and phrases. A response to a prompt by determining the next word based on the context then repeats the process until a stop condition is met. As a result, ChatGPT can produce various logical responses to various queries and prompts. The most important components of ChatGPT are the Transformer model and the language model.

Coming to the Transformer model, it is a neural network architecture designed to process sequential data. It consists of multiple layers of self-attention and a feedforward network. After the Transformer model has processed the input, a decoder generates the output. The decoder uses the context provided by the encoder to generate the response. The model is trained using unsupervised learning and fine-tuned on specific tasks using supervised learning. For successful completion of tasks, it needs pre-trained data. The model first encodes the input text, then converts it to a numerical representation, which can be processed by the model's neural network. This encoding is done using the embedded layer that maps the work.

Then comes the language model; ChatGPT is trained as a language model, trained to predict the next word in a sequence given the previous words. The language model intends to produce rational, consistent, and meaningful output. The pre-trained ChatGPT can be tuned for a specific task. So, ChatGPT passes a fine-tuning test by answering questions, generating text summaries, or generating text in response to a query. Overall, ChatGPT is a powerful language model with a combination of techniques like deep learning, machine learning, neural networks, and natural language processing.

Can ChatGPT change a wide variety of business tasks? John asks. ChatGPT possesses the ability to automate content creation on social media, create chatbots and e-commerce sites, provide medical assistance by acting as a symptom checker, write code, and assist a developer. Thus, ChatGPT can change the working of every industry. Now, John can resolve any coding issue without looking into any other resources.

But like any other technology, ChatGPT comes with a few limitations that can be its Achilles heel. ChatGPT is capable of developing content up to 2021. It finds difficulty in providing logical reasoning in certain situations and also, ChatGPT lags in translation, summarization, and sometimes question answering. But above all, ChatGPT has shown remarkable ability by providing accurate answers flawlessly in a creative way in very short periods of time.
