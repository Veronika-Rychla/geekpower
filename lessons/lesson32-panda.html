Lesson 32

Grammar - Pomnožnost a kolektivnost

Tak trochu bokem od počitatelných a nepočitatelných podstatných jmen stojí pomnožná podstatná jména. Ta jsou ve skutečnosti hned dvojího druhu. 1. Pomnožná podstatná jména v množném čísle odkazující na předměty složené ze dvou částí. 2. Kolektivní podstatná jména v jednotném čísle, ale odkazující na větší skupinu.

👓 Předměty složené ze dvou částí

Existuje spousta předmětů, které jsou složené ze dvou částí. I když tyto dvě části tvoří celek, pocit složenosti přetrvává, a tak jsou tato podstatná jména v množném čísle. Kvůli shodě podmětu a přísudku je i korespondující sloveso v plurálu a to i v případě, kdy myslíme jen jeden předmět.

👖 Oblečení: trousers / pants, jeans, shorts, tights (punčochy), leggings, overalls (montérky), pyjamas, braces (kšandy nebo rovnátka)

🧩 Trousers have evolved significantly in style over the decades.

🧩 His trousers are dirty.

🧩 Leggings are a popular choice for both exercise and casual wear.

🧩 Overalls are often worn by painters and construction workers for protection.

🧩 Pyjamas are not just for sleeping; many people wear them for lounging at home.

🎧 Brýle a sluchátka: glasses / sunglasses, goggles (ochranné brýle), binoculars (dalekohled), headpohones, speakers

🧩 Glasses are essential for many people to see clearly.

🧩 Where are my glasses?

🧩 Goggles are a must-have for protecting your eyes during lab experiments or while swimming.

🧩 Binoculars are perfect for bird watching; they bring distant objects so much closer.

🧩 Headphones are my go-to for immersing myself in music without disturbing others.

🧩 Speakers are set up around the room to create a surround sound experience for the movie night.

✂ Nástroje: pliers (kleště), tweezers (pinzeta), scales (váha), handcuffs (pouta)

🧩 Pliers are often used in electrical work to twist and cut wires.

🧩 Tweezers are essential for precision tasks like plucking eyebrows or handling small components in electronics.

🧩 Scales are crucial in laboratories for accurately measuring chemical compounds.

🧩 Handcuffs are used by law enforcement to detain suspects securely.

🏞 Ostatní: Outskirts (předměstí, okraje města), premises (prostory, areál), surroundings (okolí), goods (zboží), remains (zbytky, pozůstatky)

🧩 Outskirts are often quieter and less crowded, offering a more relaxed lifestyle compared to the city center.

🧩 Our company premises are equipped with security cameras to ensure the safety of everyone inside.

🧩 Surroundings greatly influence a person's mood and productivity, which is why office aesthetics are taken seriously.

🧩 Goods were damaged during transit, leading to a significant loss for the retailer.

🧩 Remains of an ancient civilization were discovered during the archaeological dig.

❗ Pozor na slova končící -ics

Slova jako economics, mathematics a gymnastics končí na -s, protože byla odvozena z řečtiny, odkud angličtina převzala mnoho vědeckých a technických termínů. V řečtině byla tato slova často chápána jako součást většího vědeckého celku a byly původně množné. I když je však angličtina převzala v tomto množném tvaru, tato slovíčka se v angličtině pojí s jednotným tvarem slovesa.😅

🧩 Mathematics is a fundamental subject in education.

🧩 Physics deals with the properties of matter and energy.

🧩 Economics examines how societies use resources to produce goods and services.

🧩 Ethics is the study of moral principles.

🧩 Linguistics explores the structure and function of language.

🧩 Politics influences almost every aspect of society.

🧩 Aesthetics is about the nature of beauty and art.

🧩 Dynamics is important in understanding the forces and motions that affect objects.

🧩 Mechanics is the branch of physics that deals with motion and forces.

🧩 Genetics plays a key role in determining the traits of organisms.

🧩 Acoustics studies sound and its properties.

🧩 Optics is concerned with the behavior and properties of light.

🧩 Thermodynamics examines the relationships between heat, work, temperature, and energy.

😩 Uff, jako vždy vyjímky potvrzují pravidlo

Existuje pár slovíček, která mají jen jeden tvar - a to množný - a přitom se mohou vyskytovat s jednotným tvarem slovesa i množným podle potřeby.

🦥 Species

Refers to a distinct type of living organism.

🧩 This species is endangered.

🧩 Several species were observed in the new habitat.

🚎 Means

Can refer to a method or way of doing something, or to financial resources.

🧩 In the past, the only means of communication was a handwritten letter.

🧩 Various means of transportation are available in Prague.

📺 Series

A number of things, events, or people of a similar kind or related coming one after another.

🧩 This series of lectures has been very enlightening.

🧩 She watched several series on the streaming platform.

🏢 Headquarters

The place from which an organization is controlled or directed.

🧩 The company's headquarters is in New York.

🧩 The United Nations has headquarters in multiple countries.

💡 I když slovíčko "news" se také vyskytuje vždy v množném čísle, ve skutečnosti jde o nepočitatelné podstatné jméno, a tak se pojí s jednotným tvarem slovesa, jakkoli nám to připadá neintuitivní, protože v češtině nám v množném slovesu nic nebrání. 🙄

🧩 The news was really depressing today.

🧩 The news of the ChatGPT update excites the tech community.

🧩 News about the cybersecurity breach has raised concerns among users.

🧩 The latest news from the tech conference includes a breakthrough in artificial intelligence.

🤔 A co kolektivní podstatná jména?

Kolektivní podstatná jména (collective nouns) označují skupiny lidí považovaných za celek. I když se mluví o více jedincích, tato podstatná jména se obvykle používají v jednotném čísle, protože se odkazuje na skupinu jako na jednotnou entitu.

company, staff, team, class, family, audience, committee, crew, orchestra, choir, panel (group of experts gathered to discuss a topic)

Nicméně v závislosti na kontextu a záměru mluvčího mohou být s těmito podstatnými jmény spojována jak slovesa v jednotném, tak ve množném čísle. Rozhodování mezi jednotným a množným číslem závisí na tom, zda mluvčí považuje skupinu za jednotný celek (= pak je použito sloveso v singuláru) nebo se zaměřuje na individuální členy této skupiny (= pak je použito sloveso v plurálu).

🧩 My family is big.

🧩 My family are all doctors.

🧩 The team is winning.

🧩 The team are playing their best.

🧩 The government is implementing new policies.

🧩 The government are divided on this issue.

🧩 The class is learning English.

🧩 The class have different opinions.

🧩 The audience is excited.

🧩 The audience are clapping their hands.

🧩 The choir sang beautifully at the concert.

🧩 The orchestra performed a stunning concert of Beethoven's Symphony.

🧩 The panel of judges will make the final decision

🧩 The film crew worked long hours to finish the shooting on time.

Jako vždy se však najde vyjímka. 😅 V angličtině je slovo "police" vždy používáno s množným přísudkem, protože odkazuje na skupinu jednotlivců tvořících policejní sílu a ne na jednotlivou entitu. Pokud bychom chtěli odkázat na jednotlivého policistu, musíme použít slova jako "a police officer, 👮‍♂️ a policeman, 👮‍♀️ a policewoman."

Co se týče použití členu, slovo "police" obvykle nevyžaduje "the" při odkazování na policejní sílu obecně, jako v případě 🧩 "Police are here to maintain order." Nicméně, určitý člen "the" se může použít, když se odkazujeme na konkrétní jednotku policie nebo když se slovo "police" používá v určitějším nebo specifičtějším kontextu, jako například 🧩 "The police force has received new funding."

🧩 Police have issued a warning about the recent thefts.

🧩 The police are investigating the incident.

🧩 I see a police officer over there. Let's ask her.

Vocabulary - Pomnožné IT koncepty

I když některé z těchto termínů mohou mít v odlišných kontextech i jednotné číslo, jsou často používány v množném čísle, aby odkazovaly na soubor prvků nebo činností souvisejících s IT.

📑 Credentials /krɪˈdenʃəlz/

In IT, credentials refer to the verification data required to authenticate the identity of a user or device. This often includes usernames, passwords, digital certificates, or keys.

🧩 You need to update your login credentials to access the new software update.

🧩 The system stores all user credentials securely to prevent unauthorized access.

🧩 After entering my credentials, I was able to access the encrypted files.

📑 Settings

Settings in IT refer to the options or configurations that users can adjust to control the behavior of a software application, operating system, or device.

🧩 Go to the settings menu to adjust the screen brightness and volume.

🧩 Users can customize their experience by changing the app's settings.

🧩 The default settings can be modified to optimize performance.

📑 Communications

Refers to the various methods and technologies used for transmitting information between systems or devices.

🧩 Upgrading our communications infrastructure improved data transfer speeds.

🧩 Effective communications protocols are essential for secure network operations.

🧩 The team is troubleshooting an issue with satellite communications.

📑 Operations

In IT, operations often refer to the day-to-day activities and tasks necessary for the maintenance and management of IT systems and services.

🧩 IT operations are critical for the smooth running of our online services.

🧩 The operations team implemented a new deployment strategy.

🧩 Monitoring systems are essential for efficient IT operations management.

❗ Jak již bylo zmíněno, pozor však na odborné termíny odkazující na vědecké disciplíny. V takovém případě jde o převzaté řecké názvy a ty se pojí s jednotným tvarem slovesa.

📑 Analytics

Analytics in IT involves the collection, analysis, and interpretation of data to uncover patterns, trends, and insights, often used to inform decision-making or improve systems.

🧩 Our team uses web analytics to understand user behavior on our site.

🧩 Data analytics tools help us predict customer trends and improve our services.

🧩 The analytics report showed an increase in app engagement after the last update.

📑 Diagnostics

Diagnostics in IT refer to the processes and tools used to identify and resolve issues within software, hardware, or networks, often through systematic testing and analysis.

🧩 The diagnostics tool pinpointed the network failure within minutes.

🧩 Running a full diagnostics scan can help identify the cause of the system crash.

🧩 The technician used advanced diagnostics to troubleshoot the server errors.

📑 Statistics

Statistics in IT involve the collection, analysis, and interpretation of data related to computer-based systems and their performance.

🧩 Network usage statistics help us plan for capacity upgrades.

🧩 The software generates performance statistics for each module.

🧩 Analyzing these statistics can help improve system efficiency.

📑 Electronics

Electronics in IT refers to the circuits, devices, and systems used in computers and communication technologies.

🧩 Advances in electronics have miniaturized many IT devices.

🧩 The team is experimenting with flexible electronics for wearable devices.

🧩 Sustainable electronics is becoming a priority in IT manufacturing.

📑 Robotics

The field of engineering and science that involves the design, construction, operation and use of robots.

🧩 Robotics has revolutionized manufacturing by improving efficiency and safety on production lines.

🧩 The team's latest project in robotics involves developing a robot that can assist in disaster relief efforts.

🧩 Advancements in robotics are paving the way for autonomous vehicles and drones.
