Lesson 13

Grammar - Spontánnost a váhání

Už jsme si řekli, že present continuous používáme pro pevné plány a be going to + infinitiv pro naše záměry. Jak je to ale s našimi spontánními rozhodnutími? Tady konečně přichází ke slovu ono oblíbené a studenty často nadužívané slovíčko will.

Nejspíš vás překvapí, že will je ve skutečnosti modálním slovesem stejně jako should, have to, must, can a could, o kterých ještě bude řeč v budoucích lekcích. Pro výukové účely se však vyjádřením budoucnosti pomocí will říká future simple a učí se separátně od ostatních modálních sloves.

🧨 Když se rozhodujeme okamžitě

Pokud učiníme rozhodnutí, které jsme si nemohli promyslet předem, takzvaně se rozhodujeme na místě teď a tady, použijeme právě will.

🧩 Okay, I'll fix that bug immediately. Give me two hours.

🧩 This data is messy. I will clean it up before the meeting.

🧩 There are too many security risks so I will change my password immediately.

🧩 I've just realized we're not backing this up. I will set up a backup system today.

🧩 This old version is causing too many issues. I will upgrade to the latest one.

🧩 After seeing these analytics, I will create a new BI dashboard for better visualization.

🧩 Given the feedback, we won't release the feature this week.

💪 Nabídky pomoci, souhlasy, žádání a sliby chyby

Další relativně často používanou skupinku činností, kde se používá will, jsou nabídky pomoci, okamžité souhlasy, když někoho o něco žádáme a slibování.

🧩 Slib: Don't worry, I'll fix that bug tomorrow. = pokud váš kolega použije will místo going to, tak to možná nemyslí tak úplně vážně aneb sliby chyby 🤣

🧩 Nabídka pomoci: That code looks too complicated. I'll help you. = máme laskavého seniorního kolegu 🥰

🧩 Souhlas: Can you tell the boss that I'm leaving earlier? Sure, I'll tell her.

🧩 Žádání v otázce: Will you please help me with this BI dashboard?

🧨 Pokud si nejsme jistí a váháme

Váhání je dalším skvělým případem, kdy můžeme dát budoucnosti s will zazářit. Pokud váháme, tak nemáme pevný plán a často dokonce ani silný záměr. Možná něco budeme dělat nebo taky ne. Pro tyto účely se hodí váhání zkombinovat se slovíčky jako think, wonder, probably, (not) sure, protože danou věc zvažujeme.

🧩 I don't think I'll install that software update yet. I've heard it has some bugs.

🧩 Given the recent data breaches, I probably won't use that cloud service for now.

🧩 I don't think I'll change to the new platform immediately. I need to understand its features better.

🧩 After seeing these reviews, I think I won't purchase that BI tool just yet.

🧩 I'm not sure about the compatibility. I don't think I'll upgrade the system right now.

🧩 I've been considering it, but I probably I won't attend the IT conference this year.

🧩 With all these technical issues, I'm not sure sure I'll recommend this software to the team.

🧩 Given the costs, we won't invest in that new data analytics platform for now.

🧩 I hope that our new app will be huge success.

🧩 I wonder when real virtual reality will come to the market.

🙄 Predikce bez důkazů

Již jsme si ukázali, že se k predikci používá going to + sloveso. Co tady najednou dělá will? Predikce však mohou být dvojího typu: 1. Máme důkaz a tak můžeme učinit informovanou predikci -> be going to + sloveso. 2. Hádáme a taháme z paty, máme jen takový názor nebo doufáme -> will.

🧩 See those black clouds? It's going to rain, we have to hurry. = důkazem jsou černé mraky

🧩 Don't worry, I'm sure she will pass that C# exam. = I když ve větě zazní "I'm sure," ve skutečnosti si nemůžeme být jistí a jen druhou osobu uklidňujeme.

🧩 He won't be hired for that job, he doesn't have enough work experience. = mluvíme o kamarádovi, ale třeba na pohovoru oslnil a firma mu dá šanci

🧩 The office looks completely different now. You won't recognise it! = možná jen přehání

🤔 Jak se z toho nezbláznit?

Nyní máme již tři způsoby, jak vyjádřit budoucnost. Jak mezi nimi snadno rozlišit a nezbláznit se z toho? Představte si, že si chcete dát nějaký oběd. V závislosti na tom, jak moc máte svůj oběd naplánovaný nebo spontánní, použijeme jiné způsoby, jak tuto jistotu nebo naopak nejistotu vyjádřit.

🧩 I'm having sushi for lunch. 💯 = sushi už je předem objednané, takže ho určitě budu mít

🧩 I'm going to have sushi for lunch. 💭 = Celé dopoledne myslíme na to, že si chceme dát sushi a máme na něj chuť, ale nemáme ho objednáno. Může se stát, že sushi chef s naší oblíbené restauraci bude nemocný nebo nás nakonec zláká něco jiného.

🧩 I'll have sushi for lunch. 💬 = Sedíme v restaurace nad jídelním lístkem a vybrali jsme si spontánně.

🧩 OpenAI is launching DALL-E 3 for their subscribers this October. = oznámili to jako pevný plán

🧩 OpenAI is going to launch voice functionality next year. = mají to v úmyslu a už na tom pracují, ale jestli a kdy to opravdu bude, je zatím nejisté

🧩 OpenAI will launch their intelligent rocket to Mars in 2033. = mé zbožné přání/predikce

Vocabulary - Očekávání nadvakrát

Když máme očekávání, je to také určitý druh nejisté budoucnosti nebo predikce. Například se na něco těšíme, s napjetím to očekáváme nebo naopak máme obavu, že něco nebude tak, jak jsme doufali. K vyjádření očekávání existují v angličtině dvě slovíčk,a ač jsou si významově dost podobná, protože obě vyjadřují situaci, kdy s napjetím vyhlížíme budoucnost, mají růzdílné nuance.

Anticipation /ˌek.spekˈteɪ.ʃən/ - emotivní

Anticipation vyjadřuje očekávání, které je spojeno s emocionálním nadšením. Nejde o to, že jen na něco čekáme, vyloženě se na to těšíme a nemůže se dočkat.

🧩 I'm full of anticipation for the new game release, so I will download it as soon as it's available.

🧩 In anticipation of the webinar, I will set a reminder so I don't forget to join.

🧩 In anticipation of the new Apple product, a lot of people are saving up money to buy it.

🧩 I can't wait for the virtual reality headset to arrive. I will rearrange my room in anticipation.

🧩 In anticipation of the tech conference next month, I will review the agenda and plan my schedule.

🧩 Anticipation for the new coding course is high, so I will sign up.

Expectation /ænˌtɪs.ɪˈpeɪ.ʃən/ - rozumové

Expectation je rozumová a logická záležitost, často podložená určitou formou důkazu nebo minulé zkušenosti.

🧩 Based on previous releases, the IT department has the expectation that server load will increase by 20%.

🧩 The expectation is that the new app will be very popular.

🧩 We have an expectation that the website will get more visitors next month.

🧩 My expectation is that the software update will fix the bug.

🧩 Our expectation is that the new servers will handle traffic more efficiently.

🧩 I have the expectation that my computer will be faster after the upgrade.

Obě tato slovíčka mají i svou slovesnou variantu - anticipate a expect. Narozdíl od slovesa expect však sloveso anticipate maličko mění zabarvení a již často nevyjadřuje radostné nadšení a může být tedy zaměněno s expect. Pokud chcete i nadále nadšení vyjádřit, uchylte se k k ustáleným vazbám jako can't wait for, look forward to, be eager a excited about.

🧩 They anticipate/expect that the release will be delayed.

🧩 We don't anticipate/expect any trouble.

🧩 I expect that the online course will improve my coding skills.

🧩 The company expects that the new security measures will protect data better.

🧩 The team expects that the project will be completed by the deadline.

🧩 I will sign up for the new coding course, as I highly anticipate it.

🧩 I can't wait for the online sale, so I'm making a list of things I'll buy.

🧩 I'm really looking forward to the new coding course.

🧩 I'm excited about the tech conference next month, so I'll review the agenda and plan my schedule.

🧩 I'm eager to learn more about cybersecurity.

🧩 I'm looking forward to using the new photo editing app, so I'll take some photos to edit.

Listening - AI ve velmi blízké budoucnosti

Researchers are preparing us for a revolution that will change the world forever by looking into the future of artificial intelligence. This revolution isn't going to happen in a few decades but in a few years, which means that the clock is ticking for now for businesses to start embracing AI. Artificial intelligence has become a part of our lives. Due to its revolutionary nature, artificial intelligence also faces numerous debates regarding potential impacts on individuals. Although it could be risky, it's also a fantastic opportunity.

It is estimated that the global artificial intelligence market will reach 267 billion dollars by 2027. People are worrying about losing their jobs as AI continues to advance because it has revolutionized industries across all sectors. But AI has increased the number of jobs and possibilities available to individuals across all industries. Every machine needs a person to run it. Although AI has replaced certain occupations, it still creates more jobs for humans.

AI can be categorized into three types. Firstly, weak AI performs specific tasks like Apple's Siri. General AI performs tasks like a human. Super AI is more capable than a human, but both General AI and super AI are hypothetical because these technologies are not yet developed and research is ongoing. However, the development of such AI will take a lot of time. The development of AGI will accelerate withing the following ten years. In fact, according to researchers, there is a 25% probability that by 2023, AI will resemble humans when it comes to thinking capacity. Additionally, the current data boom, computer developments, and improvements in automated methods and machine algorithms will provide a solid foundation for platforms with human-level AI.

AI has occupied crucial sectors like healthcare, cybersecurity, e-commerce, agriculture, robotics, education, the military system, finance, gaming and many more. AI is used in healthcare to identify diseases, helping healthcare service providers and their patients make better treatment and lifestyle decisions. Without AI, we couldn't have made the incredible recent advancements in fields like robotics, virtual reality, chatbots, face recognition and autonomous vehicles. Smart robot advisors in finance, insurance, legal, media and journalism will deliver instantaneous research or discoveries.

In the upcoming years, it is anticipated that artificial intelligence and machine learning will become more prominent in the consumer market, which will immediately affect how well web development functions. Virtual assistants or chatbots will provide professional advice. Other advantages include strengthening governance through better decision-making processes, shortening time to market for R&D initiatives and streamlining transportation and supply chain networks. Given all these scenarios, AI is all set to revolutionize every sector in the near future. Given that, it is expected to impact 60% of firms and will continue to spread deeper into a wide range of industries in the upcoming years.

Currently, driving development in our smart devices, cars, and favorite apps. In the coming years, AI will invade every aspect of our life and eventually surpass human intellectual capacity. It won't be long before artificial intelligence becomes a common part of our everyday lives. As it becomes more prevalent, it will allow us to live more efficiently and improve our lives. By increasing their effectiveness, efficiency and concentration on the primary tasks that require people's attention, AI technologies are transforming many industry sectors. Artificial intelligence will change the world for the better.

Writing - Pište o nejasné budoucnosti

📝 Napište 10 vět na libovolné kombinace s will - nejasná budoucnost, spontánní rozhodnutí, predikce bez důkazů, sliby, váhání apod.

🧩 I don't think I'll go abroad this year.

🧩 I hope that my friend will get a new job as a tester soon.

🧩 I don't expect the next testing case will go smoothly.

🧩 I expect the release will be on time.

🧩 I think that AI will get real intelligence by 2050.
