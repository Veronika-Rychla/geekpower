Lesson 24

Grammar - Jak je to s tím would

S pomocným slovesem "would" se studenti setkávají na každodenní bázi a přitom je to právě toto sloveso, které mnoho lidí používá tak nějak intuitivne, často špatně, bez hlubšího pochopení jeho významu a funkce. Pojďme se proto podívat, k čemu se skutečně používá, a že vůbec není tak záhadné, jak se na první pohled jeví. Obecně si would lze představit jaké české "by, bych, bychom, bysme, byste."

✨ Podmíněné věty

if + past simple , would + sloveso v základním tvaru

Již jsme si ukázali, že "would" se používá k vyjádření výsledku hypotetické nebo představované situace. V tomto případě pomocné sloveso "would" pomáhá slovesu, které po něm následuje, vyjádřit hypotetickou situace, kdy "by" se něco stalo, pokud by byla podmínka naplněna.

🧩 If we had more computing power, we would run more complex algorithms.

🧩 If the system were more efficient, it would process requests faster.

🧩 If I knew C#, I would programme my own game.

💎 Hypotetické situace

would + sloveso v základním tvaru

Hypotetické situace nemusí vždy zahrnovat klasické podmínky za pomocí "if," často se chceme druhé osoby zeptat, jak by se hypoteticky zachovala v situaci, která sice reálně nenastala, ale zajímá nás druhého názor či reakce.

🧩 How would the system behave under extreme load?

🧩 What features would you include in a dream application?

🧩 How would an AI change our daily IT operations?

🧩 What security measures would be necessary for a fully online workplace?

Protože otázky jsou hypotetické, budou hypotetické i odpovědi na tyto otázky.

🧩 Under extreme load, the system would become very slow and might crash.

🧩 In a dream application, I would include voice recognition and automatic translation.

🧩 An AI would automate routine tasks and improve data analysis in our IT operations.

🧩 For a fully online workplace, we would need strong passwords and two-factor authentication.

⚡ Přání a touhy

would + často like / love / prefer / rather

"Would" se používá k vyjádření přání nebo tužeb, zejména když nejsou pravděpodobné, že se splní.

🧩 I would love to develop a groundbreaking AI algorithm.

🧩 She would prefer to work on open-source projects.

🧩 We would like to have a faster internet connection in our office.

🧩 He would rather use Linux than Windows for his work.

🙏 Zdvořilé žádosti nebo nabídky

"Would" se používá k vyjádření zdvořilých žádostí nebo nabídek.

🧩 Would you like me to review your code?

🧩 Would it be possible to get more storage space?

🧩 I would like to request access to the database.

🧩 Would you prefer to use this software or that one?

📅 Would v minulosti

would + have + past participle

Použití "would" v minulosti jsme si už ukázali při tvorbě třetí podmínkové věty. Podmiňovací způsob pro minulé události se používá, když potřebujeme vyjádřit lítost nad tím, že se něco stalo či nestalo.

🧩 The team would have met the deadline if they had more resources.

🧩 We would have avoided the data breach if we had updated our security software.

🧩 If the database had been backed up regularly, we would have easily restored the lost data.

Would v minulosti však samozřejmě není omezené jen na třetí podmínku.

🧩 I don't know how I would have finished that presentation in just two hours without ChatGPT.

🧩 It's a pity we didn't go with that solution. I'm sure our servers would have handled the load.

🧩 She's an expert programmer so she would have solved that problem easily by herself. Instead, we outsourced it and wasted money.

🧩 I would have called the product owner, but I didn't have his number.

🧩 I'm happy that they gave that task to my senior. I wouldn't have been able to solve it.

🧩 We should have hired a professional. A technician would have repaired the server by now.

🤔 Would nebo will?

Pro vyjádření blízké budoucnosti se může použít would i will, rozdíl je v našem záměru. Pokud jsme danou věc schopni udělat a nic nám v tom nebrání a jsme rozhodnutí ji udělat, použijeme will. Pokud "bychom" něco sice teoreticky mohli udělat, ale něco nám v tom brání, takže nemůžeme, použijeme would.

🧩 I'll fix that bug immediately, I have time.

🧩 I would fix that bug immediately, but I have a meeting in ten minutes.

🧩 I'll call the system administrator, I have his number.

🧩 I would call the system administrator, but I don't have his number.

💨 Zkrácené tvary

would not = wouldn't / would = 'd

Would má zkrácené tvary nejen pro svůj záporný tvar, ale i pro svou kladnou podobu. U kladné podobny je třeba si dát pozor na to, že tato zkrácená forma může v některých kontextem být ve skutečnosti zkrácené "had" a vyjadřovat předminulý čas.

🧩 I'd fix that bug immediately, but I have a meeting in ten minutes.

🧩 I wouldn't update it now if I were you. It can take half an hour.

🧩 She'd like to learn more about IT. = she would like

🧩 She'd finished the report. = she had finished (past perfect)

💡 Srovnej: has X is

Tento jev se projevuje stejně jako v případě zkráceného 's.

🧩 She's writing a report. = she IS writing

🧩 She's finished that report. = she HAS finished

Vocabulary - Video buttons

▶ Play Button

Usually represented by a right-pointing triangle. It starts the playback of the video.

⏸ Pause Button

Displayed as two vertical lines. It stops the video playback temporarily while allowing you to resume from the same point.

⏹ Stop Button

Often represented by a square. It stops the playback and may reset the video to the beginning.

⏮ Rewind Button

Shown as an arrow pointing leftward, sometimes doubled. It moves the video backward at various speeds.

⏭ Fast Forward Button

Depicted as an arrow pointing rightward, and can also be doubled. It moves the video forward at different speeds.

⏩ Next Button

Usually a right-pointing arrow with a vertical line at the end. It skips to the next video or next chapter in the video.

⏪ Previous Button

A left-pointing arrow with a vertical line at its beginning. It goes back to the previous video or chapter.

🔊 Volume Control

Often represented by a speaker icon. It adjusts the volume level, sometimes with additional icons like muted speaker or sound waves to indicate volume level.

🔳 Full Screen Button

Typically displayed as two diagonal arrows pointing outward or a rectangle icon. It enlarges the video to fill the entire screen.

⚙ Settings Button

Often depicted as a gear icon. It opens a menu for various settings like video quality, subtitles and more.

💬 Closed Caption/Subtitles Button

Usually shown as "CC" or a speech bubble. It toggles the display of subtitles or closed captions.

🔛 Progress Bar

A bar that shows the current position and length of the video. You can drag a handle or click on the bar to jump to different parts of the video.

Listening - What if we created a superintelligence?

We heard the warnings. Elon Musk: "I tried to convince people to slow down. Slow down AI. I tried for years." But we didn't listen. Stephen Hawking: "The development of full artificial intelligence could spell the end of the human race." Now we live in a world controlled by artificial intelligence. How did we let this happen? Will we be able to survive? And is this the end of the world? This is WHAT IF and here's what would happen if we created superintelligence.

The days with AI like Siri and Alexa, that made our lives better, are now over. We no longer control AI; in fact, if it wants to, it can control us. With a superintelligence being created, there's no telling what it would do. One thing we do know is that this AI would be far smarter than any human being. It would quickly create new technologies that humans today could never even dream of.

But wait, how exactly did we get here? The very first digital computer was invented in 1937 as a way to help humanity. And for the most part, computers and AI have given us a lot of good. In addition to giving us smartphones, the internet and video game consoles, it also allowed us to explore the galaxy, make new medicines and even robots.

Human intelligence led to the birth of technology. But as we continue to improve artificial intelligence, AI may become smart enough to start running itself. Technology will become so advanced that it won't need humans to operate it anymore, becoming uncontrollable.

As AI becomes free to do whatever it pleases, it will continue to improve on itself - becoming more advanced with every passing second. This is known as a technological singularity. This technological singularity may allow AI to do almost anything, even physically. Such as robots taking over our manufacturing machines.

When that happens, they can create their own machines. And let's be honest, by the time something like this happens, humans will most likely have created an AI that can make its own physical objects. If something like this happens, it can pretty much only mean bad things for us humans. Unfortunately, this is more of a WHEN IF rather than a WHAT IF.

Since the 1980s, thought leaders in AI and technology have been predicting that it could happen. Some think it could even happen by the middle of this century. If it did happen, what would it mean for us as human beings? Well, that would really be up to our AI overlords. In this world, humans would essentially be useless bags of meat taking up space. Insulting, I know, but if AI took control of everything, we really wouldn't be all that useful. Anything that the AI wanted to do or accomplish would be slower and less efficient if done by humans.

But if the AI were to somehow allow us to live among them, then what would happen? Well, let's hope they wouldn't pave over our crops to build more robot factories. Since AI doesn't need food, they most likely wouldn't care about our feeble human needs. Robot 1: "And what do humans do with these things?" Robot 2: "Oh man, what didn't they do, they bounced them."

Unless they allowed us to continue to manufacture and grow our own food, it would deplete over time, leaving us humans with nothing. The only reason AI would keep us humans alive is if they somehow liked us enough to keep us as what would essentially be pets. We could spend time with them, keep them company, maybe even cuddle with them, just like our pets do for us. A life like this wouldn't be so bad. Hanging out at home all day, being well fed, not really having a care in the world.

That would probably be the best bet for humanity, because there's virtually no chance of us overruling a superintelligence in our current state. One way we could actually compete with this AI is if we fused our bodies with technology and became cyborgs. Allowing the human race a different kind of evolution, one that includes artificial intelligence. But could we really do that? That sounds like a story for another WHAT IF.

Writing - Bych, bychom, byste

📝 Napište 10 vět s použitím would s různými použitími: podmínky, přání, minulost, nabídka atd. Kde to jde, použijte zkrácené tvary.

🧩 Cloud storage would have been a good solution, but the company didn't have money to pay for it.

🧩 I wouldn't use that framework if I were you, it's no longer supported.
