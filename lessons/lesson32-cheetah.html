Lesson 32

Grammar - Členy věčné to téma

Vocabulary - IT odvětví

✨ Coding /ˈkəʊ.dɪŋ/ Programming /ˈprəʊɡræmɪŋ/

The process of writing instructions for computers using programming languages that tell a computer how to perform a task.

🧩 She spent the weekend coding a new feature for the app.

🧩 The project requires coding in multiple programming languages.

🧩 Programming requires both logical thinking and creativity.

🧩 He's learning programming with a focus on web development.

✨ Testing

The process of exploring a program or application with the intent of finding software bugs.

🧩 Automated testing tools can significantly accelerate software development.

🧩 The quality assurance team is responsible for testing every aspect of the product.

✨ Data Analysis

The process of inspecting, cleansing, transforming, and modeling data with the goal of discovering useful information, informing conclusions, and supporting decision-making.

🧩 Data analysis techniques can reveal trends and patterns that inform strategic business decisions.

🧩 Visualization tools play a crucial role in making the results of data analysis accessible.

✨ Cybersecurity

The practice of protecting computers, servers, mobile devices, electronic systems, networks, and data from malicious attacks, damage, and unauthorized access.

🧩 Employees received training on cybersecurity to reduce the risk of phishing attacks.

🧩 Investing in cybersecurity is essential for data protection.

✨ Data Science

A multidisciplinary field that uses scientific methods, processes, algorithms, and systems to extract knowledge and insights from structured and unstructured data.

🧩 She is pursuing a career in data science.

🧩 Data science is transforming the way companies understand their customers' behavior.

✨ Network Administration

The process of managing and maintaining the hardware and software that comprise a computer network.

🧩 The network administration team is responsible for ensuring 24/7 network availability.

🧩 Network administration involves setting up firewalls and routers to secure data.

✨ Database Management

The practice of storing, organizing, and managing data using database management systems to ensure data availability, security, and integrity.

🧩 Effective database management is key to handling large volumes of data efficiently.

🧩 Regular database backups are a critical part of database management to prevent data loss.

✨ DevOps

A set of practices that combines software development (Dev) and IT operations (Ops), aiming to shorten the system development lifecycle and provide continuous delivery with high software quality.

🧩 Adopting DevOps practices has enabled faster software releases with fewer errors.

🧩 The DevOps team worked on automating the deployment process.

✨ AI and Machine Learning

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence in machines designed to think and learn like humans. Machine Learning (ML), a subset of AI, focuses on the development of algorithms that enable computers to learn from and make decisions based on data.

🧩 Researchers in AI and machine learning are exploring new ways to automate complex decision-making processes.

🧩 Machine learning algorithms can improve over time, learning from new data and user interactions.

✨ UX and UI Design

User Experience (UX) design is the process of creating products that provide meaningful and relevant experiences to users. User Interface (UI) design focuses on the visual and interactive elements of a product's interface.

🧩 Good UX and UI design can significantly enhance user satisfaction and engagement with an app.

🧩 A good integration of UX and UI design principles is key to creating successful digital products.

✨ IT Support and Helpdesk

IT Support and Helpdesk services provide assistance to individuals and organizations in resolving technical problems or queries related to computer systems, software and hardware.

🧩 Users can contact the helpdesk via phone or email for assistance with software installation.

🧩 The IT support team quickly resolved the issue.

✨ Digital Marketing

Digital marketing is the component of marketing that utilizes digital media and platforms to promote products and services.

🧩 The digital marketing strategy includes social media, email campaigns, and SEO to increase online visibility./p>

🧩 Analytics tools are essential for measuring the success of digital marketing efforts.

✨ Business Intelligence

Business Intelligence (BI) involves technologies, applications, strategies, and practices used to collect, analyze, integrate, and present business information. The purpose of BI is to support better business decision-making.

🧩 Business intelligence tools help companies transform data into actionable insights.

🧩 The BI dashboard provides a real-time overview of the company's financial performance.

✨ Project Management in IT

Project management in IT involves planning for the completion of an IT project, often utilising agile and SCRUM methodologies.

🧩 Agile methodologies are often used in IT project management to accommodate changing requirements.

🧩 The project manager oversees the IT project from the beginning to completion, ensuring it meets all specifications.

Vocabulary - Stages of software development

✨ Business Analysis

This initial stage involves understanding the business needs, objectives, and challenges that the software aims to address. Business analysts work closely with stakeholders to gather requirements and set clear goals for the project.

🧩 During the business analysis phase, the team identified key requirements for the software.

🧩 The business analysis resulted in a requirements document that guided the entire software development process.

🧩 Business analysts ensure that all parties agreed on the scope and vision of the project.

✨ Designing (UX/UI Design)

This stage involves creating the overall design of the software, focusing on user experience (UX) and user interface (UI). It's about understanding the users' needs and ensuring the software is intuitive, accessible, and visually appealing.

🧩 The design team created wireframes to outline the software's UI.

🧩 User feedback was crucial in refining the UX/UI design for better usability.

🧩 The UX/UI design phase ensured the software was both functional and visually engaging.

✨ Development /dɪˈvel.əp.mənt/ vývoj

This is the core stage where the actual software is developed. Programming and coding involve translating the software design into a functional program using programming languages.

🧩 Agile methodologies are often used in software development.

🧩 The development team is working on a new mobile application.

🧩 Pair programming helped improve code quality and foster collaboration among developers.

✨ Testing

Testing is critical to identify and fix bugs or defects in the software. This stage verifies that the software meets all requirements and works as expected under various conditions.

🧩 Testing is a crucial phase in the software development lifecycle.

🧩 Automated testing can improve efficiency and coverage.

🧩 Performance testing evaluated the software's behavior under load.

✨ Debugging /ˈdiːˌbʌɡ.ɪŋ/ ladění

The process of identifying, analyzing, and removing errors or bugs from software or hardware.

🧩 Debugging complex code requires patience and experience.

🧩 The development team spent several hours debugging the new software release.

✨ Deployment /dɪˈplɔɪ.mənt/ nasazení

The process of distributing and installing a software application to a computing environment where it can be executed.

🧩 The final step in the project is the deployment of the application to production servers.

🧩 Deployment strategies vary depending on the complexity of the application.

🧩 Beta releases allowed for real-world testing before the final deployment.

✨ Support and maintenance

After deployment, ongoing support and maintenance are crucial to address any arising issues, provide user support, and release updates for improvements and new features.

🧩 Customer support plays a crucial role in user satisfaction with software products.

🧩 The IT department provides technical support to all employees.

🧩 Regular software updates included bug fixes, security patches and new features.

Vocabulary - Make your data safe

✨ Content /ˈkɒntɛnt/ obsah

The information that is directed toward an end-user or audience.

🧩 The website's content is regularly updated to engage users.

🧩 Creating quality content is essential for successful digital marketing.

✨ Storage /ˈstɔːrɪdʒ/ - uložiště

Storing data for future use.

🧩 Cloud storage offers a flexible solution for data management.

🧩 Secure data storage is a top priority for the IT department.

✨ Backup /ˈbæk.ʌp/ záloha

🧩 The IT department performs a full system backup every weekend.

🧩 Regular data backup is crucial for disaster recovery planning.

✨ Recovery /rɪˈkʌv.ər.i/ obnova

The process of restoring lost, deleted, or corrupted data from backup storage.

🧩 Data recovery efforts were successful after the system crash.

🧩 The recovery plan includes steps for restoring services after an outage.

✨ Data Migration

The process of transferring data between storage types, formats, or computer systems.

🧩 The company initiated a data migration project to move all legacy data to a new cloud-based storage system.

🧩 During the data migration process, special care was taken to ensure no data was lost or corrupted.

✨ Data Archiving

The process of moving data that is no longer actively used to a separate storage device for long-term retention. Archived data is kept for future reference or regulatory compliance needs.

🧩 Data archiving helps the company reduce storage costs by moving older, less frequently accessed data to more cost-effective storage solutions.

🧩 The archived data was indexed and cataloged to ensure quick retrieval.

✨ Data Privacy

Practices and policies that ensure sensitive data, such as personal information, is handled securely, in compliance with privacy laws.

🧩 Employees received training on data privacy to ensure they understood how to handle customer information securely.

🧩 In response to growing concerns about data privacy, the software company introduced enhanced encryption and user authentication features.
